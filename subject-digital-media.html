Digital Media

With the help of Digital Media module I improved my knowledge about multimedia and how it works. I found a lot of interesting information about images, sounds, animation and videos. Now I know how they were created and what lies in the basis of each of these multimedia sources.

I was always interested in image editing. A few attempts to learn Photoshop from video tutorials didn`t go further than cutting out image and pasting it on new layer. But on Digital Media labs I learned how to edit images in Gimp and it turned out to be easier than it seemed to be. Step by step tutorials made a huge difference and helped me to improve my image editing skills.

Overall I learned how to use :

  • Gimp - image editing
  • OBS studio - screen recording
  • Audacity - sound
