Maths

Math is not something I was enjoying throughout my life. My mother was a maths teacher but she did not like helping me with this subject. Therefore I was solving mathematical problems by myself just using books and any theoretical or practical information that I could find. This helped me to develop math problem solving skills and it is extremely handy now.

Software development math course consists of difficult subsections:

  1. Differential Calculus
  2. Taylor Series
  3. Matrices
  4. Vectors in 2D and 3D
  5. Integral Calculus
  6. Complex Numbers
  7. Statistics

It is an extensive amount of math that are hard to learn in 9 months. But they are crucial for software engineers as a lot of image technologies, image compression, game engines and game physics rely on mathematical calculations behind them. That is why Maths is an important part of the course and its all relevant to subjects that will be learned in the future.
