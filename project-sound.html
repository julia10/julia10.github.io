Sound Studio

Sound editing skills are very important nowadays as a lot of digital media includes audio as a part of it. To demonstrate my editing knowledge I recorded a short text description about role of AI in today`s world (Hamilton, 2020). I made a few changes to the recording:

  1. Amplified the volume by 4 decibels

  2. Cut out breathing sounds

  3. Added a layer of office sounds

  4. Reduced the volume of office sound in relation to voice sound

  5. Added a fade in at the beginning and fade out in the end of track
