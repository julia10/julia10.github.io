TYPES OF COFFEE

Types of coffee

Here are some of the most popular coffee types drunk by irish people


Caffè Americano

An Americano is simply an espresso that has been poured over, or been diluted with hot water.

Espresso

Espresso is a full-flavored, concentrated form of coffee that is served in “shots.”

Café Latte

A caffè latte is simply a shot or two of bold, tasty espresso with fresh, sweet steamed milk over it.

Espresso macchiato

The espresso macchiato is an espresso topped with steamed milk foam

Iced Latte

A summer version of Café Latte with ice

Cappuccino

A cappuccino is an espresso-based coffee drink that is traditionally prepared with steamed milk foam (microfoam).

Flat White

It is comparable to a latte, but smaller in volume and with less microfoam.

Irish Coffee

It`s a cocktail consisting of hot coffee, Irish whiskey, and sugar, stirred, and topped with cream.
